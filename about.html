About us

The hospital was established in April 2022.The main of this hospital is to help people who are need of health care with in the yoni region and Sierra Leone as whole.

Our History

Our hospital was founded in 2022 by a group of dedicated medical professionals with a mission to provide the best possible care for their patients. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, always staying at the forefront of medical advancements and technology.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, patient-centered healthcare services that meet the needs of our community. We are committed to making sure that everyone who comes through our doors receives the best possible care, and we work tirelessly to provide a safe, welcoming, and supportive environment for our patients and their families. We are giving HOPE to Pregnant women, children, and people in need around the communities we serve, by motivating and providing charitable interventions. Health for all.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of medical services, including emergency care, surgery, physical therapy, diagnostic imaging, and pharmacy. Our team of highly skilled doctors and staff are dedicated to improving the health and well-being of our patients.
